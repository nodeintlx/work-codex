## Program 1: BDC Inclusive Entrepreneurship Loan (PRIMARY)

**What:** BDC's $250M program for businesses 51%+ owned by Black, Indigenous, or women entrepreneurs.

**Amount:** Up to $350,000

**Terms:**
- Variable interest rate (floating base + individual variance)
- Principal payments can be deferred up to 24 months
- No application fee, no annual fee
- Completing BDC's Entrepreneurship Fundamentals online course earns additional interest rate reduction

**Eligibility — NRG Bloom qualifies:**
- Canadian incorporated — YES
- 51%+ owned by Black entrepreneur — YES
- Revenue under $3M — YES

**How to apply:**
1. Call **1-877-232-2269**
2. Say: "I am a Black entrepreneur applying for the Inclusive Entrepreneurship Loan. My business is a federally incorporated Canadian company in Montreal, early stage, in clean technology and modular data centers for West Africa. I want to confirm eligibility and get assigned to a specialist."
3. BDC specialist walks through application
4. Submit documentation
5. Complete Entrepreneurship Fundamentals course (for rate reduction)
6. Funds disbursed

**URL:** bdc.ca/en/financing/inclusive-entrepreneurship-loan

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from application to disbursement

---

## Program 2: FACE Black Entrepreneurship Loan Fund (PARALLEL)

**What:** Government of Canada + BDC + major banks partnership. $291M total fund. Administered by FACE (Federation of African Canadian Economics).

**Amount:** Up to $250,000 (FACE handles up to $100K directly; BDC underwrites above $100K)

**Eligibility:**
- Self-identify as Black — YES
- Canadian citizen/PR or Canadian legal entity — YES
- 18+ years old — YES
- For-profit business — YES
- Startups eligible — YES (projections accepted instead of financials)

**How to apply:**
- Apply at **facecoalition.com/en/loans**
- Or email **info@facecoalition.com**

**Can you apply to both BDC and FACE simultaneously?** Yes — different programs, different administrators. Disclose both applications to each lender.

**Timeline:** 3-6 weeks

## Risk Flag

BDC Inclusive Entrepreneurship Loan's exact revenue floor is not publicly specified — only a $3M ceiling. BDC may ask for evidence of business activity (contracts, LOIs, site work). The Oando engagement and Nigeria operations documentation should be assembled as supporting evidence of traction.

## What NOT To Do
