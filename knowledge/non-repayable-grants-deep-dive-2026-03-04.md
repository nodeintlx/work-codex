## TIER 2: APPLY THIS MONTH — High Value Grants

### 6. NRC IRAP (Industrial Research Assistance Program)
- **Amount:** Up to $10M (typical $100K–$500K)
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE contribution
- **What:** R&D, technology development, commercialization for SMEs
- **Hidden benefit:** They assign you a FREE Industrial Technology Advisor (ITA) who proactively identifies ALL programs you qualify for
- **April 1 = new fiscal year = fresh budget**
- **Action:** Call 1-877-994-4727
- **Priority:** HIGH — one call unlocks multiple funding streams

### 7. Clean Growth Hub (Free Federal Program Mapping)
- **Amount:** Free service
- **What:** Federal government maps your company to every eligible program
- **Action:** Book call at ised-isde.canada.ca/site/clean-growth-hub/en
- **Why most people miss it:** Nobody knows it exists

### 8. SR&ED + Quebec CRIC (Retroactive Tax Credits)
- **Amount:** $100K–$500K+ cash refund for FY2023-2024
- **Type:** Refundable tax credit = cash back from government
- **Combined rate:** Up to 65% refund on qualifying R&D spend
- **Key insight:** IQ bridge financing gets you the cash within weeks of filing
- **Action:** Find SR&ED specialist (Leyton didn't answer, Ayming didn't understand — try other firms)
- **Other SR&ED firms to try:** G6 Consulting (Montreal), Northbridge Consultants, RDBASE

### 9. Digital Skills for Youth (DS4Y)
- **Amount:** Up to $30,000 per intern (government reimburses wages)
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE — government pays you to hire a recent grad
- **How:** Hire an underemployed post-secondary graduate (age 15-30), get reimbursed
- **Through ECO Canada (clean tech focus):** Up to $18,000 reimbursed (80% of costs)
- **Action:** Contact ECO Canada at eco.ca — apply BEFORE hiring
- **Why most people miss it:** Buried in ISED programs, delivered through third parties

### 10. PRIIME (Quebec Immigrant Integration Subsidy)
- **Amount:** Quebec pays up to 60% of employee's gross salary for 40 weeks
- **Plus:** Up to $5,000 for HR adaptation + $2,000 for mentoring
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE wage subsidy
- **Who qualifies:** Hire an immigrant (PR under 5 years) or visible minority
- **Net impact:** A $60K salary hire = ~$36K reimbursed. Hiring for $24K net for 10 months.
- **Action:** Call Services Québec 1-888-643-4721 — apply BEFORE hiring
- **Why most people miss it:** Candidate typically initiates at Services Québec office

### 11. Emploi-Québec PAÉE (Employer Hiring Subsidy)
- **Amount:** 50% of wages for 12-52 weeks
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE wage subsidy
- **Who qualifies:** Individuals with employment barriers (long-term unemployed, older workers)
- **Action:** Contact local Centre local d'emploi (CLE) via Services Québec
- **Note:** Cannot stack with PRIIME on same employee

### 12. Scale AI — AI Training Grant
- **Amount:** Up to $1,000,000
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE
- **What:** Custom AI training development (lectures, coaching, workshops)
- **Rolling applications — no deadline**
- **NRG Bloom/Coldstorm angle:** Coldstorm AI delivers custom AI training = direct fit
- **Action:** Join Thursday 3pm ET info session at scaleai.ca/projects/
- **Requires:** Quebec NEQ number

### 13. Mitacs Accelerate / BSI
- **Your cost:** $5,000–$7,500 per intern → Mitacs matches → $10,000–$15,000 total
- **Type:** Cost-shared — you pay half, government pays half
- **Requires:** University partner (faculty supervisor)
- **Strategy:** Approach professor at Concordia, HEC, McGill, or Polytechnique
- **Project ideas:** "West Africa energy market entry analysis" or "AI-powered DC optimization"
- **Action:** Contact Mitacs Montreal Advisor at mitacs.ca

### 14. CanExport Innovation
- **Amount:** Up to $75,000 (75% of eligible costs)
- **Type:** NON-REPAYABLE grant
- **What:** International R&D partnerships
- **Revenue threshold:** $100K (lower than CanExport SMEs)
- **Opens:** March 2026
- **Action:** Prepare application

### 15. CanExport SMEs
