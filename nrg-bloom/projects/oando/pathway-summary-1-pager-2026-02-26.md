### The Opportunity

NRG Bloom deploys modular data centers at Oando's upstream sites, converting stranded or flared gas into **USD-denominated digital infrastructure revenue** — at **zero capital cost to Oando**.

We have been operational at Ogboinbiri (Bayelsa State) since February 2025, proving this model works in the Niger Delta environment.

We present **three partnership structures** — each designed for a different set of operational and regulatory conditions. Oando selects the pathway that fits.

---

### Three Pathways at a Glance

|                              | **Pathway 1**                        | **Pathway 2**                        | **Pathway 3**                        |
|------------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Structure**                | Power Purchase Agreement (PPA)       | Joint Infrastructure (PPA)           | Gas Purchase Agreement (GPA)         |
| **Description**              | NRG Bloom buys excess power from existing Ogboinbiri gensets | Oando installs dedicated genset; NRG Bloom buys the power | NRG Bloom buys raw gas and deploys its own generation + data center |
| **Oando Provides**           | Excess electrical capacity (~5 MW)   | Dedicated gas genset (~5 MW)         | Associated gas supply (from flare stream) |
| **NRG Bloom Provides**       | Full data center ($1.5–2.0M capex)   | Full data center ($1.5–2.0M capex)   | Full stack: gas reception, generation, data center ($4.0–5.5M capex) |
| **Oando Capex**              | **Zero**                             | Genset investment (to be scoped)     | **Zero**                             |
| **Fixed Rate (Option A)**    | $0.018/kWh                           | $0.027/kWh                           | $0.30–0.80/MMBtu gas price           |
| **Revenue Share (Option B)** | 30% of compute revenue               | 35% of compute revenue               | N/A — revenue via gas price          |
| **Est. Annual Revenue to Oando** | ~$730,000–750,000 USD            | ~$1,100,000–1,150,000 USD            | Revenue via gas margin               |
| **5-Year Revenue to Oando**  | ~$3.65–3.75M USD                     | ~$5.5–5.75M USD                      | Depends on volumes & pricing         |
| **Community Impact**         | None — excess only; community supply preserved | None — separate dedicated infrastructure | None — gas sourced from flare, not community power |
| **Key Condition**            | Requires confirmation that excess capacity is not encumbered by HCDT/PIA obligations | Community power remains on separate infrastructure | Gas composition data required for equipment sizing |
| **Risk to Oando**            | Zero capital risk                    | Modest genset investment; de-risked by NRG Bloom's long-term offtake | Zero capital, zero infrastructure risk |
| **ESG / Regulatory**         | Monetizes wasted capacity            | Creates new dedicated commercial arrangement | Strongest NGFCP compliance; direct flare elimination |
| **Scalability**              | Limited to Ogboinbiri excess         | Replicable with new gensets at other sites | Most scalable — deployable at any Oando flare site |
| **Complexity**               | Simplest                             | Moderate                             | Most complex for NRG Bloom; simplest for Oando |
