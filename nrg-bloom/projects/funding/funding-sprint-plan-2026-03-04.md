## TIER 1: Apply Today / This Week (1-6 weeks to cash)

### 1. BDC Inclusive Entrepreneurship Loan
- **Amount:** Up to $350K
- **Speed:** 2-4 weeks
- **Why this one:** Designed for Black entrepreneurs, no minimum revenue floor, startups eligible with projections
- **Action:** Call 1-877-232-2269 — ask for Inclusive Entrepreneurship Loan specialist
- **Docs needed:** Certificate of Incorporation, photo ID, business plan/projections, shareholder info, void cheque
- **URL:** bdc.ca/en/financing/inclusive-entrepreneurship-loan
- **Note:** Do NOT apply for the general Small Business Loan — requires 24+ months revenue

### 2. FACE Black Entrepreneurship Loan Fund
- **Amount:** Up to $250K (FACE handles up to $100K directly; BDC underwrites above $100K)
- **Speed:** 3-6 weeks
- **Why:** Explicitly accepts startups with projections, no revenue minimum
- **Action:** Apply at facecoalition.com/en/loans or email info@facecoalition.com
- **Docs needed:** Same package as BDC above
- **Can apply in parallel with BDC:** Yes — different programs, different administrators. Disclose both.

### 3. Afro-Entrepreneurs Fund (Quebec-specific)
- **Amount:** $50K–$500K
- **Speed:** 3-6 weeks
- **Why:** Purpose-built for Black Quebec entrepreneurs
- **Action:** Call 1-888-525-2075
- **URL:** Check current status — relatively new program

### 4. RBC Black Entrepreneur Business Loan (BEBL)
- **Amount:** Up to $250K
- **Speed:** 3-6 weeks
- **Action:** Visit RBC branch or call to request Black entrepreneur lending specialist

### 5. PME MTL
- **Amount:** Up to $300K loans + $60K clean tech grant + $15K young business grant
- **Speed:** 4-8 weeks
- **Action:** Book meeting at nearest PME MTL hub
- **URL:** pmemtl.com/en

## TIER 2: High Value, Needs Setup (1-3 months)

### 6. SR&ED + Quebec CRIC (Retroactive Tax Credits) + IQ Bridge Financing
- **Amount:** $100K–$500K+ cash refund for FY2023-2024 R&D
- **Speed:** Normally 3-6 months — BUT Investissement Quebec offers bridge financing (up to 100% of expected refund, disbursed within weeks of filing)
- **Why this is the highest-value single action:** Contingency-fee consultants = $0 upfront risk, combined federal+Quebec rate = up to 65% refund on R&D spend
- **Action:** Call Leyton (514-767-1227) and Ayming (514-931-0166) THIS WEEK
- **Key insight:** Once SR&ED/CRIC is filed, apply for IQ bridge financing to get cash immediately instead of waiting 6 months for CRA/RQ to process

### 7. NRC IRAP
- **Amount:** Up to $10M (typical $100K-$500K)
- **Speed:** 6-12 weeks
- **Why:** Non-repayable contributions for R&D. April 1 = new fiscal year = fresh budget
- **Action:** Call 1-877-994-4727 for Montreal-area Industrial Technology Advisor
- **URL:** nrc.canada.ca/en/support-technology-innovation

### 8. Clean Growth Hub
- **Amount:** Free — maps you to every eligible program
- **Speed:** Immediate
- **Action:** Book a call — they do the program-matching work for you
- **URL:** ised-isde.canada.ca/site/clean-growth-hub/en

### 9. CSBFP (Canada Small Business Financing Program)
- **Amount:** Up to $1.15M (government-backed loan through your bank)
- **Speed:** 2-4 weeks
- **Action:** Walk into Desjardins or your bank and request CSBFP application
- **Note:** Up to $500K for equipment/leasehold, $150K for intangibles. Government guarantees 85%.

## TIER 3: Larger but Longer (3-6 months)

### 10. CanExport Innovation
- **Amount:** Up to $75K (75% of eligible costs)
- **Deadline:** Opens March 1, 2026
- **Why:** Lower revenue threshold ($100K) than CanExport SMEs ($300K)
- **Action:** Prepare application — international R&D partnerships

### 11. Investissement Quebec PSCE (Export Support)
- **Amount:** Up to $500K per project, $500K per company total
- **Action:** Contact IQ Montreal office
- **URL:** investquebec.com

### 12. CanExport SMEs
- **Amount:** Up to $50K (50% of costs)
- **Deadline:** May 29, 2026
- **Requires:** Certificate of Incorporation + GST 34 return + $300K revenue
- **Action:** Register on tradecommissioner.gc.ca portal

### 13. EDC (Export Development Canada)
- **Products:** TELP (working capital guarantee), Political Risk Insurance (90% Nigeria coverage)
- **Action:** Call 1-800-229-0575 — ask for Trade Advisor for Sub-Saharan Africa
- **Note:** OVERDUE — was due Feb 28

### 14. Technoclimat (Quebec)
- **Amount:** Up to $3M (50% of costs)
- **Requires:** Quebec demonstration site
- **Action:** Periodic calls — check MERN for next intake
