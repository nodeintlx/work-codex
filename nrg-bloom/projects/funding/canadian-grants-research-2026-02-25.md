## SECTION 3: FEDERAL TAX CREDITS

### SR&ED (Scientific Research & Experimental Development)
- **Amount:** 35% refundable ITC on first $6M of qualifying R&D (expanded from $3M, Dec 2024); 15% on excess
- **What:** Tax credits for R&D labour, materials, contractors, overhead
- **Eligibility:** Any CCPC conducting eligible SR&ED in Canada
- **Deadline:** 18 months from end of tax year — 2024 work must be filed by June 30, 2026
- **Backdatable:** YES — file retroactively for 2023 and 2024
- **Relevance:** HIGH — potentially $100K–$500K+ in cash refunds
- **SR&ED consultants work on CONTINGENCY (15–25% of refund, no upfront cost)**

### Quebec CRIC (Research, Innovation & Commercialization Credit)
- **Amount:** 30% refundable on first $1M; 20% above $1M. Fully refundable.
- **What:** Stacks on top of federal SR&ED. Combined = up to 65% refund on R&D spend.
- **Eligibility:** Corporation in Quebec, R&D/pre-commercialization in Quebec
- **Deadline:** Aligned with corporate tax filing
- **Relevance:** HIGH — stacks with SR&ED

### Clean Technology Investment Tax Credit
- **Amount:** 30% refundable on eligible clean tech property
- **What:** Solar, wind, storage, heat pumps, ZEVs purchased in Canada
- **Note:** Fossil fuel exclusion risk for flare gas — consult tax advisor
- **Relevance:** LOW-MEDIUM
