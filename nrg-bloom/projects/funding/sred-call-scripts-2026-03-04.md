## Call 1: Leyton — (514) 767-1227

### Opening
"Hi, I'm Makir Volcy, CEO of NRG Bloom Inc. We're a federally incorporated Canadian company based in Montreal. We design and deploy modular data centers for emerging markets — specifically containerized computing infrastructure powered by stranded gas and flare gas in West Africa. I'm calling to understand whether an SR&ED specialist could capture more value from our R&D activities than our generalist accountant."

### Key Questions to Ask

**1. Qualifying Activities**
"Can you help me understand what would qualify as SR&ED in our case? Our R&D activities include:
- Designing modular, containerized data center systems (the 'Kifaru' design) — thermal management, power systems, compute density optimization for tropical climates
- Energy system integration — converting stranded gas and flare gas to power for computing loads
- Developing deployment methodologies for infrastructure in emerging markets with unreliable grid power
- Software and automation for remote monitoring and operations of distributed sites

Would these activities qualify?"

**2. Value Gap**
"Our generalist accountant is handling our T2 and CO-17 filings. How much do companies typically leave on the table when a generalist prepares SR&ED claims versus a specialist? What's the typical uplift?"

**3. Review / Audit Capability**
"Can you review what our accountant has filed or is filing, to see if we're capturing everything? Or do you need to start from scratch?"

**4. Quebec CRIC**
"Are we getting the Quebec R&D tax credit (CRIC) on top of federal SR&ED? The combined rate should be up to 65% — I want to make sure both are being claimed."

**5. FY2024 Optimization**
"Our FY2024 SR&ED deadline is June 30, 2026. Can you still optimize this year's filing? And can you file retroactively for FY2023 as well?"

**6. Fee Structure**
"Do you work on contingency? Specifically, I'm interested in a model where your fee is a percentage of the incremental recovery — meaning what you capture above and beyond what our accountant would have filed. That way there's zero risk to us."

**7. IQ Bridge Financing**
"I understand Investissement Quebec offers bridge financing against expected SR&ED and CRIC refunds. Have you helped clients access this? How does the process work — do you provide the documentation IQ needs?"

**8. Timeline**
"If we engage you today, what's the realistic timeline to:
(a) Complete the assessment of qualifying activities
(b) File the optimized claim
(c) Receive the refund (or access IQ bridge financing)"

### Close
"What do you need from me to get started? Can we schedule a more detailed assessment call this week?"

**Get:** Contact name, email, next steps, any intake form they send.
