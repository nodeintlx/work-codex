# Draft 03
## Short-Form Video Script
## What People Misunderstand About Building In Nigeria

### Hook

What people misunderstand about building in Nigeria is that progress is not just about having a good plan.

### Body

You can have the numbers.
You can have the deck.
You can have the technical model.

And still be completely unprepared for what execution actually demands.

Because in environments like Nigeria, people need to see movement.

They need to see action.
They need to see responsiveness.
They need to feel that things are actually moving in the real world, not just inside meetings.

That means trust matters.
Presence matters.
Timing matters.
Relationships matter.

And if you underestimate those things, the project starts breaking long before the spreadsheets show it.

### Close

That’s one of the biggest lessons hard execution taught me:
projects don’t fail only because of technology.
They fail because people misunderstand reality on the ground.

### Caption Option

Building in Nigeria taught me that execution is physical, relational, and visible. If people can’t see movement, trust erodes fast.
