# Litigation Narrative Framework

Purpose: preserve the full case story in a disciplined way for counsel, filings, mediation, and later witness preparation.

This is not for public release.

## Core Theory

NRG Bloom originated, built, and operationally sustained the Nigeria opportunity and site pathway. TON progressively devalued NRG Bloom's position, exploited NRG Bloom's work and information, delayed and underperformed in ways that destabilized local conditions, then terminated NRG Bloom at the point where the project was becoming monetizable and moved to capture the benefit.

## Main Story Arc

### 1. Origination

- Makir identified the opportunity
- Makir invited TON into it
- NRG Bloom created the initial path and local access
- NRG Bloom introduced the key relationships and local credibility

### 2. Build Phase

- NRG Bloom did the real on-the-ground work
- site development, logistics, community management, transport, security, power coordination, and operational continuity were driven by NRG Bloom
- Makir documented and marketed the work as part of building project momentum

### 3. Progressive Devaluation

- TON repeatedly changed economic structure and bargaining posture
- threats and restructuring reduced NRG Bloom's expected value
- NRG Bloom kept performing despite worsening terms

### 4. Delay and Destabilization

- TON's delays and failures made local management harder
- local frustration and community anger increased
- the burden of holding the situation together fell disproportionately on NRG Bloom
- NRG Bloom worked to preserve status quo while TON's conduct made that harder

### 5. Termination and Appropriation

- TON terminated NRG Bloom before NRG Bloom could realize the value of the work
- after termination, the site appears to have restarted mining on March 6, 2026
- NRG Bloom lost visibility and likely lost revenue access after the pool/wallet change
- this supports a live appropriation and monetization theory

## What Must Be Proven Carefully

These themes are strong, but require disciplined proof:

- TON delays materially worsened community conditions
- NRG Bloom bore the reputational and operational cost of those delays
- TON's conduct was not just sloppy but strategically self-serving
- TON benefited from work product, local positioning, and site buildout created by NRG Bloom
- TON terminated at the monetization inflection point

## Evidence Buckets For This Narrative

### A. Origination / Introducer Proof

- emails with Axxela
- WhatsApp coordination
- chronology of meetings and introductions
- pitch or model materials created in the early phase

### B. Operational Contribution Proof

- site photos and videos
- travel evidence
- expenditure ledger
- security/community/logistics records
- project coordination chats

### C. Delay / Burden / Status-Quo Proof

- payment delay evidence
- community escalation messages
- remediation and site issue communications
- evidence Makir was mediating local crises

### D. Appropriation / Benefit Capture Proof

- termination timing
- March 6 pool spike and later visibility loss
- witness statements about transformer commissioning
- evidence TON resumed operations after cutting NRG Bloom out

## Litigation Use Cases

Use this narrative for:

- counsel memos
- claim drafting
- mediation preparation
- witness outlines
- chronology synthesis
- damages framing

Do not use it for:

- social posts
- public interviews
- broad public statements during active proceedings

## Key Discipline Rules

1. Distinguish observed fact from inference.
2. Distinguish motive theory from proven act.
3. Use documents, messages, and media as support, not emotional decoration.
4. Avoid overstating what the evidence does not yet prove.
5. Preserve the strongest media assets as evidence of contribution and authorship, not just publicity material.

## Current Strong Framing

The disciplined current framing is:

- NRG Bloom built substantial value
- TON benefited from that value
- NRG Bloom was pushed out before monetization
- TON appears to have resumed monetization after termination
- NRG Bloom should not bear the cost of having created value that TON then captured

## Current Caution

Do not yet convert these into categorical public accusations unless evidence is locked down:

- "TON intentionally turned the community against NRG Bloom"
- "TON planned every delay for takeover purposes"
- "TON stole the site"

Those may become argument themes later, but for now they should remain litigation theories backed by evidence, not public slogans.
