## 2. NEW SECTION: B.4 — THE AXXELA CIRCUMVENTION

This warrants separate treatment because of its scale and the strength of the documentary evidence.

**The Axxela opportunity is valued at a minimum of US$15.3 million over five years** based on financial projections jointly developed by both parties. NRG Bloom originated, developed, and negotiated this opportunity over five months before TON systematically excluded NRG Bloom and engaged Axxela directly.

**Key facts, documented in writing:**

- **February 4, 2025:** Axxela cold-emailed NRG Bloom directly (info@nrgbloom.com). TON had zero involvement in originating this relationship.
- **February 7 and 25, 2025:** NRG Bloom met Axxela at their Lagos offices — first Makir alone, then presenting to Franklin Umole (MD). Axxela's own meeting minutes describe TON as "investors from Canada" who were "invited to see" NRG Bloom's facility.
- **April 22, 2025:** Makir proposed the $0.03/kWh rate to Axxela — the exact rate that became the confirmed deal price. TON was not on any of these emails.
- **June 12, 2025:** Axxela confirmed the $0.03/kWh rate to both Tyler and Makir — acknowledging both parties as equal partners.
- **June 24–July 2, 2025 (9-day circumvention window):**
  - June 24: David Billay (TON) takes over Axxela correspondence. Makir removed from all emails.
  - June 25: TON sends DD checklist and Gantt chart to Axxela. Makir not included.
  - June 27: Draft PPA sent to TON only. NRG Bloom excluded from the key document.
  - July 2: Axxela follows up with TON. Makir still excluded.
  - July 3: Nana (Axxela Head of BD) re-includes Makir, requests both company profiles for Board approval.
- **July 4, 2025:** Marley emails Franklin Umole: "NRG Bloom is fully aware." This statement was **provably false** — Makir had been excluded from PPA correspondence for 9 days.
- **July 7, 2025:** Nana separately forwards the PPA to Makir — suggesting even Axxela's own staff recognised NRG Bloom had been improperly excluded.
- **July 11, 2025:** Makir discovers the circumvention and objects formally in a lawyer-approved email demanding all direct Axxela communications cease unless NRG Bloom is included.
- **September 2025:** Tyler confirms to Makir and Julie that he was about to sign the PPA with Axxela executives.

TON offered NRG Bloom a $1,000/MW finder's fee ($10,000 total) for a relationship NRG Bloom originated and developed. This offer is equivalent to 0.065% of the projected deal value.

**NRG Bloom's introducer role is established beyond dispute by Axxela's own records.** The circumvention is documented email-by-email. Marley's false representation to Axxela is documented in writing. NRG Bloom's immediate objection is documented in writing with lawyer's approval.

**The JV Agreement's Section 5 exclusivity clause independently prohibits TON's pursuit of any Nigerian Bitcoin mining project without NRG Bloom.** TON's engagement with Axxela constitutes a direct breach of this commitment, actionable under Nigerian jurisdiction as part of the unified commercial arrangement between the parties.
