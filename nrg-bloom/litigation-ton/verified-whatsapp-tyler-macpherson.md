## 1. Methodology

This timeline was produced by reading every line of the WhatsApp chat export between Makir Volcy (CEO, NRG Bloom Inc.) and Tyler MacPherson (Founder, TON Infrastructure Ltd.), file `tyler-macpherson/_chat.txt`, consisting of 541 raw lines.

**Process:**
1. Every message, voice call, attachment, and system notification was catalogued.
2. Multi-line messages (where WhatsApp wraps content across export lines) were consolidated under the originating timestamp.
3. Each entry was assigned a litigation relevance tag from the following taxonomy:
   - **THREAT** -- Termination threats, leverage, or coercive language
   - **ADMISSION** -- Statements against TON's interest or confirming NRG Bloom's claims
   - **CIRCUMVENTION** -- Evidence of going around NRG Bloom to deal directly with introduced parties
   - **FINANCIAL** -- Revenue share, payments, pricing, cost discussions
   - **DEVALUATION** -- Reduction of NRG Bloom's role, terms, or compensation
   - **TERMINATION** -- Formal or informal termination notices
   - **AXXELA_DEAL** -- References to Axxela, the 10MW opportunity, or the gas-to-power deal
   - **OPERATIONAL** -- Site logistics, equipment, technical specifications
   - **RELATIONSHIP** -- Trust-building, promises, personal rapport
   - **LOGISTICS** -- Travel coordination, scheduling, non-substantive coordination
   - **NOT_RELEVANT** -- Social banter, media shares, no litigation value
4. Exact quotes are preserved verbatim, including typos.
5. Voice calls are noted with duration; calls around key dates are flagged.

**Accuracy note:** All timestamps are as-is from the WhatsApp export. Times appear to be in Eastern Time based on Makir's timezone. Tyler is in Mountain Time (Alberta), meaning his local time is 2 hours behind the timestamps shown.
