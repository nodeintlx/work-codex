## Executive Summary

The Axxela opportunity is worth an estimated **$15.3 million over five years** and represents the single most valuable commercial relationship in the TON-NRG Bloom partnership. NRG Bloom originated, developed, and negotiated this opportunity over **five months** (February - June 2025) before TON progressively circumvented NRG Bloom starting in late June 2025.

The evidence establishes:
1. **Axxela contacted NRG Bloom directly** on February 4, 2025 -- a cold inbound inquiry
2. **NRG Bloom built the entire relationship** from first email through commercial term negotiation
3. **NRG Bloom proposed the $0.03/kWh rate** that became the deal price
4. **TON was brought in later** (May-June 2025) as NRG Bloom's partner
5. **TON systematically excluded NRG Bloom** from PPA correspondence starting June 24, 2025
6. **Marley Broda falsely represented** NRG Bloom's awareness to Axxela on July 4, 2025
7. **NRG Bloom objected immediately** upon discovery on July 11, 2025

April 22, 2025: Makir proposes "$0.03 per kWh or 35% of the hashrate/revenue." This exact $0.03 rate became the confirmed deal price on June 12.

### 5. NRG Bloom was the primary negotiating party for 4+ months
Feb 4 → Jun 12: NRG Bloom led every commercial exchange. TON appeared on Axxela emails only when NRG Bloom brought them in as a deployment partner.
