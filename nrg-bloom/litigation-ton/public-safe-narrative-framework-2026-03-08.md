# Public-Safe Narrative Framework

Purpose: define what Makir can say publicly about the work, the site, and the experience without undermining the TON case.

This framework is intentionally narrower than the full truth. It is built for strategic restraint during active proceedings.

## Core Rule

Public-safe narrative is not the full dispute narrative.

It is a controlled founder/operator story that:

- preserves credibility
- avoids confidential or prejudicial detail
- avoids naming TON unless counsel approves
- protects the legal case
- still lets Makir reclaim authorship of the work and experience

## What Public-Safe Narrative Is For

- founder brand
- operator credibility
- future business development
- proof of lived execution experience
- showing that Makir built real infrastructure capability in Nigeria

## What Public-Safe Narrative Is Not For

- litigating in public
- accusing TON directly
- pressuring the mediator
- publishing confidential facts
- publishing dispute materials
- proving bad faith to the public

## Allowed Themes

These are the safest themes to build around.

### 1. Builder Narrative

Allowed:

- "I spent a year building real mining infrastructure capability in Nigeria."
- "I worked through logistics, customs, community relations, power constraints, and field execution."
- "I learned what it takes to move a project from concept to site reality."

Avoid:

- naming TON as the client/partner
- saying the site was "stolen"
- saying anyone intentionally sabotaged you

### 2. Operator Under Pressure

Allowed:

- "I kept difficult projects moving under pressure."
- "I handled delays, community friction, and execution risk on the ground."
- "I learned how fragile partnerships become when execution discipline fails."

Avoid:

- identifying the other side
- assigning motive publicly
- disclosing internal disputes or legal positions

### 3. Market/Field Insight

Allowed:

- lessons about Nigeria infrastructure projects
- lessons about local execution, logistics, trust, and timing
- lessons about power, community, and site-readiness

Avoid:

- identifying the exact site or counterparties
- disclosing commercial information from the TON matter

### 4. Creator Portfolio Narrative

Allowed:

- "I documented, filmed, and built media around high-friction industrial projects."
- "I create operational and field content around infrastructure work."

Avoid:

- reusing identifiable TON/site content without review
- implying present affiliation or endorsement

## Forbidden Public Themes For Now

Do not publish the following themes while proceedings are active:

- "TON stole my site"
- "TON used me and cut me out"
- "TON wanted the community to turn against NRG Bloom"
- "I have proof they acted in bad faith"
- anything quoting settlement or legal correspondence
- anything discussing mediation, arbitration, or litigation tactics
- anything accusing individuals of fraud, theft, or intentional sabotage without counsel-cleared strategy

## Tone Rules

Public tone should be:

- calm
- credible
- experienced
- restrained
- reflective

Public tone should not be:

- enraged
- threatening
- accusatory
- score-settling
- obviously aimed at the opposing party

## Safe Structure

Use this structure for public content:

1. what you worked on
2. what you learned
3. what kind of operator it made you
4. what kind of projects or partnerships you want next

## Safe Example Directions

Examples of content directions that are more likely to be usable:

- "What it takes to build energy-linked industrial projects in Nigeria"
- "A year of field execution taught me more than any strategy deck could"
- "Why community alignment matters as much as technical execution"
- "The hidden operational work behind infrastructure projects"
- "What I learned documenting and operating difficult projects on the ground"

## Red Flags Before Posting

Before posting, ask:

1. Does this identify TON directly or indirectly?
2. Does this reveal the site?
3. Does this reveal confidential business information?
4. Does this refer to the dispute or legal process?
5. Does this accuse anyone of intentional wrongdoing?
6. Would this look to a neutral third party like pressure on active proceedings?

If yes to any of those, do not post it yet.

## Best Current Public Lane

The best current lane is:

- founder/operator storytelling
- general infrastructure and execution insight
- generic visuals only
- no names, no accusations, no dispute framing

That lets Makir keep building public presence without weakening the case.
