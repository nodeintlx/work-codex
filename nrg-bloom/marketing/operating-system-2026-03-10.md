# NRG Bloom Content Operating System

Created: 2026-03-10
Purpose: turn founder thoughts into a repeatable content funnel that supports authority, partnerships, and future demand generation.

## Strategic Outcome

The system should help NRG Bloom do four things:

1. Turn loose ideas into publishable assets quickly.
2. Make every post serve a funnel role instead of living as a one-off.
3. Build Makir's founder authority around hard execution in West Africa.
4. Protect the company from publishing content that cuts against the live TON legal posture.

## Core Positioning

NRG Bloom should sound like:

- a builder, not a commentator
- a field operator, not a theorist
- commercially literate, not hype-driven
- future-facing, not grievance-led

The working public position is:

"We build practical infrastructure in hard environments, we learn expensive lessons in the field, and we share the operating truths that most slide decks leave out."

## Funnel Design

### Top of Funnel

Purpose:
- reach new people
- grow trust
- sharpen market positioning

Formats:
- LinkedIn essays
- X threads
- short videos
- founder observations

Angles:
- execution lessons
- Nigeria market reality
- builder credibility

### Middle of Funnel

Purpose:
- deepen trust
- prove judgment
- create reasons to start conversations

Formats:
- frameworks
- checklists
- teardown posts
- mini case studies
- email notes

Angles:
- structuring cross-border projects
- operational risk management
- partnership discipline
- power and logistics realities

### Bottom of Funnel

Purpose:
- convert attention into real business conversations
- create qualified inbound

Formats:
- consultation CTA posts
- project diagnostics
- partner-facing one-pagers
- direct offer emails

Angles:
- modular data center deployment
- stranded-energy project structuring
- West Africa execution support

## Weekly Publishing Engine

Recommended starting cadence:

- Tuesday: founder authority post on LinkedIn
- Thursday: sharper repurposed thread or carousel
- Friday: email note or short founder video

Every captured idea should produce:

1. one primary asset
2. two repurposed assets
3. one CTA
4. one follow-on idea

## Agent Workflow

### 1. Intake Agent

Input:
- raw thought
- voice note transcript
- customer question
- meeting note

Output:
- audience
- pillar
- funnel stage
- hooks
- CTA
- lead magnet
- recommended publish date

### 2. Strategy Agent

Input:
- content brief

Output:
- campaign angle
- primary post recommendation
- repurpose plan
- follow-up topic ideas

### 3. Drafting Agent

Input:
- approved brief

Output:
- LinkedIn post
- X thread
- email angle
- short video script

### 4. Editorial Agent

Input:
- queue state
- current dates

Output:
- what to post this week
- what is missing in the funnel
- what needs legal review

## What Makes This Useful

The system is only useful if it does three things well:

- classifies ideas fast enough that you actually use it
- keeps the content tied to offers and audience pains
- avoids generic AI filler by forcing NRG Bloom-specific framing

## Immediate Next Step

The March 10, 2026 MVP is:

- a brand system with audiences, pillars, and CTAs
- a queue with the next scheduled posts
- a CLI command that captures new ideas into structured briefs

That is enough to make the workflow operational before building a more autonomous version.
