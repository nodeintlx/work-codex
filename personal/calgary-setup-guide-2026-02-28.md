- Walk score: 91 (highest in Calgary)

## Residency Documentation — What the Registry Accepts

**Accepted:**
- Signed residential lease agreement (your name + Alberta address, dated within 90 days)
- Utility bill in your name at Alberta address (within 90 days)
- Bank/credit card statement at Alberta address (within 90 days)

**NOT Accepted:**
- Airbnb booking confirmation
- Photocopies
- Documents in someone else's name

**Corporate housing agreement:** Qualifies if titled as "Residential Tenancy Agreement" or "Lease Agreement." Ask your provider explicitly for this format.

**No notarization required.** Standard signed agreement is sufficient.

## Banking

**RBC Digital Choice Business Account** — $6/month, unlimited electronic transactions, strongest international wire capabilities for Africa/UAE operations. Open at a Calgary branch or online.

For international transfers (CAD/NGN, CAD/AED): Use **Wise Business** alongside your bank — 0.4-0.7% FX markup vs banks' 2-3%.
