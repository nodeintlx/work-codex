## Record Sharing — What Matters

**Quebec shares driving records with ONLY:**
- Ontario
- New York State
- Maine

**Quebec does NOT share records with:** Georgia, UAE, Thailand, Philippines, or any other country researched. Your foreign driving record is completely separate from your SAAQ record.

---

## Holding Multiple Licenses — Is It Legal?

- Getting a Georgian, Filipino, or Thai license does NOT violate Quebec law — Quebec's prohibition is on holding two **Canadian provincial** licenses simultaneously
- No country researched requires you to surrender your Quebec license (except possibly UAE — verify)
- SAAQ doesn't track or care about foreign licenses you obtain abroad
- You can legally carry both your Quebec license and a Georgian license

---

## IDP (International Driving Permit) — Get This Regardless

- $25-32 from CAA-Quebec, same-day, no appointment
- Valid in 174 countries
- Must carry with your Quebec license
- Essential for: car rentals abroad, Africa travel, Asia, Italy, Greece, Japan
- Get it before you leave Canada — cannot get one abroad
